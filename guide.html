Complete Guide

Everything you need to know about Anime Verse — from start to finish.

Getting Started
First time here? Start here
1
Open the website
Visit the Anime Verse link in your browser. The homepage will load with Top 10, Latest Releases, and Trending sections automatically.
2
Browse without login
You can browse all anime, watch episodes, and use most features without any account. Login is only needed for saving anime to your personal list.
3
Choose your theme
Tap the moon/sun icon in the top header to switch between dark and light mode. Your preference is saved automatically.
The website works best in Chrome or any modern browser. No app download needed!
Login & Profile
Create account, sign in, set photo
1
Open Login
Tap the ☰ hamburger menu (top left) → tap Login / Sign Up. A popup will appear.
2
Sign up with Email
Enter your email and a password (min 6 characters). Tap Sign Up. Your account is created instantly.
3
Already have an account?
Switch to the Login tab in the popup, enter email + password, tap Login.
4
Set your profile photo
After login, tap your profile avatar (top right corner) → Profile page opens. Tap the avatar to change your photo from gallery or paste an image URL.
5
Change display name
On the profile page, tap your name to edit it. Changes save automatically.
Your account is secured by Firebase. We never store your password in plain text.
Searching Anime
Find any anime instantly
1
Tap the Search icon
The 🔍 magnifying glass icon is in the top header. Tap it and a search bar will appear below the header.
2
Type the anime name
Start typing — results appear instantly. You can search by title or genre (e.g. "Action", "Romance").
3
Tap a result
Tap any card in the results to open that anime's detail page.
Close search by tapping the ✕ button or tapping outside the search bar.
Categories & Browsing
ALL, ANIME, MOVIE, SERIES, NEWS
1
Category pills
Below the header are filter pills: ALL, ANIME, MOVIE, NEWS, SERIES. Tap any to filter content instantly.
2
ALL — Home sections
Tapping ALL shows the homepage with Top 10 Today, Latest Releases, and Trending sections.
3
Pagination
Each section shows up to 10 items. If there are more, page numbers appear at the bottom. Tap 1, 2, 3... to see more.

Watching Anime
Episodes, seasons, and video links
1
Open an anime
Tap any anime card from the home page or search results. The anime detail page opens with banner, info, and seasons.
2
Select a Season
Tap the season tab (SEASON 1, SEASON 2...) at the bottom of the info card. The season poster and episodes will load below.
3
Select an Episode
Tap EPISODE 1, EPISODE 2... buttons. The episode links will appear below in different quality options.
4
Tap Watch Now
Tap the ▶ Watch Now button to open the watch page. Select your preferred video quality (480p, 720p, 1080p, 4K).
Some episodes use direct video links that play in-browser. Others open Telegram — see the Telegram section below.
Telegram Links
How to watch via Telegram
1
What is a Telegram link?
Some anime episodes and season packs are hosted on Telegram. When you tap a quality button (720p HD, 1080p FHD etc.), it opens Telegram directly.
2
Install Telegram first
Download Telegram from Play Store or App Store. Create a free account if you don't have one.
3
Tap the quality button
On the anime page, scroll to the episode section. Tap a quality like 1080p FHD or 720p HD — Telegram will open with the video file.
4
Play in Telegram
In Telegram, tap the video to stream it directly or tap the download button to save it for offline watching.
Quality colour guide
🟠 4K UHD — Highest quality
🟢 1080p FHD — Full HD
🔵 720p HD — Standard HD
⚫ 480p SD — Lower quality, fast load
My List
Save your favourite anime
1
Save from card
Every anime card has a ♥ heart button in the top-right corner. Tap it to save that anime to your list.
2
Save from anime page
On the anime detail page, tap ♥ Add to List button. It changes to "Saved!" when added successfully.
3
View your saved list
Tap the 🔖 bookmark icon in the top header, or open the sidebar menu and tap My List.
Without login, your list is saved on your device only. Login to sync your list across devices.
Theme & Display
Dark mode, light mode
1
Toggle theme
Tap the 🌙 moon (dark) or ☀️ sun (light) icon in the top-right header on any page to switch themes.
2
Theme is saved
Your theme preference is saved in your browser. Next time you visit, the same theme will be applied automatically.
Dark theme is easier on the eyes while watching anime at night. Light theme works great during daytime.
New Content Alerts
Red dot on category pills
1
What is the red dot?
When new anime is added to a category, a small blinking red dot 🔴 appears on that category's pill (ANIME, MOVIE etc.).
2
How to clear it
Tap that category pill to browse it. Once you visit, the dot disappears automatically — meaning you've seen the new content.
The dot remembers your last visit per category. Even after refreshing the page, it stays until you tap that category.
Terms & Legal
DMCA, rules, content policy
1
Content policy
Anime Verse is a content directory. All video links point to third-party sources. We do not host any video files directly.
2
DMCA requests
If you are a rights holder and want content removed, use the Report Issue button in the sidebar or visit the Terms page.
3
Read full terms
Scroll to the footer of the home page and tap Terms or DMCA for complete legal information.
